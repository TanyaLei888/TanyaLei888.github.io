4. Be / Have / There be
系统现在过去
BeI am; he is; you/we/they arewas / were
HaveI/you/we/they have; he hashad
There bethere is / there arethere was / there were
have = 拥有者有；there be = 某处存在。
5. 时态速查
时态公式核心感觉例句
一般现在时do / does习惯、事实、规律She works here.
现在进行时am/is/are + doing此刻正在发生We are reviewing it.
一般过去时did / was / were过去发生并结束They signed it yesterday.
过去进行时was/were + doing过去某刻正在发生I was working at 9.
一般将来时will + do预测、承诺、临时决定We will send it tomorrow.
将来进行时will be + doing将来某刻正在进行I will be meeting him at 3.
现在完成时have/has + done过去影响现在；到现在为止She has finished it.
过去完成时had + done过去的过去They had left before I arrived.
将来完成时will have + done到将来某时已完成We will have completed it by Friday.
6. 否定句 / 疑问句
类型否定疑问
BeShe is not ready.Is she ready?
一般现在She does not work.Does she work?
一般过去She did not work.Did she work?
将来She will not work.Will she work?
完成She has not worked.Has she worked?
用了 does/did 后，后面的实义动词恢复原形。
